The hippocampus surface data is put in hippocampussurface.dat file, which is a 373*30000 matrix. 373 is the sample size, and 30000 is the dimension.

The time-to-event data and clinical data are put in clinical.dat file, which is a 373*15 matrix. The first column denotes observed time, the second column denotes death or censoring, the third column is the ID, and the next 12 columns are the covariates described in the paper.
